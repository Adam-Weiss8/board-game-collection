AZUL

The Portuguese Tile Game

Select Difficulty

How to Play

Goal

Score the most points by decorating your wall with colorful tiles.

Taking Tiles

On your turn, click a factory display or the center pool, then click a color. You take all tiles of that color.

Remaining tiles from the factory go to the center.

Taking from the center for the first time gives you the First Player token (a floor penalty).

Placing Tiles

Place your tiles on a pattern line (rows 1-5 on the left side of your board). Each row can only hold one color and can't match a color already placed on the wall in that row.

Overflow tiles go to the floor line (penalties).

Scoring Each Round

When all factories are empty, completed pattern lines move a tile to your wall. Score +1 for isolated tiles, or count connected horizontal + vertical tiles.

Floor penalties: -1, -1, -2, -2, -2, -3, -3

End Game Bonuses

+2 for each completed horizontal row
+7 for each completed vertical column
+10 for each completed color (all 5 tiles)

Game End

The game ends after a round in which any player completes a full horizontal row. Highest score wins!
